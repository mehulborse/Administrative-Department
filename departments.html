Administrative Departments

Comprehensive overview of all core university administration departments

Admissions Department

Gateway to Higher Education

The Admissions Department serves as the primary entry point for prospective students, managing the entire enrollment process from application to orientation.

Application Processing

Handles student applications and manages the complete enrollment workflow

Eligibility Verification

Verifies academic credentials and validates all required documents

Entrance Coordination

Coordinates entrance tests, interviews, and admission requirements

Student Orientation

Organizes orientation programs for new students and parents

Examinations & Records Department

Academic Assessment Management

Responsible for the comprehensive management of all academic assessments, record-keeping, and certification processes.

Exam Scheduling

Plans and coordinates examination timetables and supervision

Results Management

Publishes results, maintains grade sheets, and academic records

Transcripts & Certificates

Issues official transcripts, degrees, and academic certificates

Record Maintenance

Maintains comprehensive student academic history and archives

Finance / Accounts Department

Financial Operations Management

Manages all financial aspects of the university including budgeting, fee collection, payroll, and financial planning.

Budget Planning

Strategic financial planning and budget allocation management

Fee Collection

Student fee processing, receipts, and payment management

Payroll Management

Staff salaries, benefits, and compensation administration

Scholarships

Scholarship distribution and financial aid management

Human Resources Department

People Management & Development

Dedicated to managing the university's most valuable asset - its people. Handles recruitment, development, and employee welfare.

Recruitment

Faculty and staff recruitment, selection, and onboarding processes

Performance Management

Employee performance evaluation and development programs

Employee Welfare

Health benefits, wellness programs, and employee support services

Grievance Handling

Conflict resolution and employee grievance management

IT & Infrastructure Department

Technology & Digital Services

Ensures seamless technology operations across campus, managing networks, systems, and digital infrastructure.

Network Management

Campus networking, internet connectivity, and Wi-Fi services

Computer Labs

Maintenance and management of computer laboratories and equipment

System Administration

ERP systems, LMS platforms, and academic software management

Data Security

Cybersecurity, data protection, and backup management

Library Administration

Knowledge Resource Management

Manages comprehensive library services, from traditional book lending to modern digital resources and research support.

Cataloging Services

Book cataloging, classification, and inventory management

Digital Library

E-books, online journals, and digital resource management

User Support

Research assistance, user training, and information literacy

Collection Management

Acquisition, preservation, and maintenance of library collections

Facility Management

Campus Infrastructure & Maintenance

Ensures optimal campus infrastructure through comprehensive maintenance, cleanliness, and facility management services.

Infrastructure Maintenance

Building maintenance, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems

Campus Cleanliness

Sanitation services, waste management, and campus hygiene

Event Management

Event setup, logistics coordination, and facility booking

Sustainability

Green initiatives, energy management, and environmental programs

Transport / Logistics

Campus Mobility Solutions

Provides comprehensive transportation services for students and staff, ensuring safe and efficient campus mobility.

Bus Services

Daily bus routes, scheduling, and transportation for students and staff

Vehicle Maintenance

Fleet management, regular maintenance, and safety inspections

Travel Coordination

Faculty travel arrangements and official visit coordination

Route Planning

Efficient route planning and transportation logistics optimization

Public Relations / Communications

Brand & Communication Management

Manages the university's public image, communications, and stakeholder relationships through strategic outreach and media management.

Media Relations

Press releases, media coverage, and journalism coordination

Digital Presence

Website management, social media, and online brand presence

Internal Communications

Campus newsletters, announcements, and internal messaging

Event Documentation

Photography, videography, and event coverage services

Legal / Compliance

Regulatory & Legal Affairs

Ensures institutional compliance with regulatory requirements and manages legal affairs to protect university interests.

Regulatory Compliance

UGC, AICTE, NAAC guidelines adherence and regulatory reporting

Contract Management

Legal documentation, contracts, and agreement management

RTI Responses

Right to Information requests and transparency compliance

Audit Management

Internal and external audit coordination and compliance monitoring
